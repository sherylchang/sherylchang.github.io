Hi! I'm Sheryl.
I've always been a creative at heart. As a child, I loved to draw and paint, but I wasn't confident artistically.
Then, in college, I was lucky enough to take a student-led web design class, which exposed me to the world of design for the first time. I later TA'd for this class, led web design teams in Innovative Design, and took various design classes to build my skillset.
To cultivate the design sensibility I developed in college, I worked as a part-time Product Design Intern at a startup called Devie and as a Design Intern at OWIU Design. I've also made a few zines since graduating from college. But I want to make an impact with design full-time, which is why I'm actively looking to move into a design-focused, creatively stimulating role.
In my free time, I've been learning how to play tennis, doing yoga at Kefi Yoga, and taking dance classes at Epicenter. I've also been getting into anime recently, and I'm currently watching Jujutsu Kaisen. Generally, I really enjoy watching horror/monster TV shows, like "All of Us are Dead" and "Gyeongseong Creature". I love meeting new people, so feel free to reach out: sheryl.chang@berkeley.edu :-)
