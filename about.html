Hi! I'm Sheryl.
I've always been a creative at heart. As a child, I loved to draw and paint, but I wasn't confident artistically.
Then, in college, I was lucky enough to take the Web Design DeCal, which exposed me to the world of design for the first time. I later TA'd for this class, led web design teams in Innovative Design, and took various design classes to build my skillset.
I'm currently working as a Project Coordinator at Genentech. Although I love the company's mission, it has been very difficult for me not to be doing design work there and not to be challenged creatively. To continue to cultivate the design sensibility I developed in college, I have been working as a Product Design Intern part-time for the past year and have made a few zines since graduating from college. But I want to make an impact with design full-time, which is why I'm actively looking to move into a design-focused, creatively stimulating role.
In my free time, I've been having outdoor picnics with my friends, making 年糕 (Chinese mochi cake), and reading Sapiens with my cousin. I love meeting new people, so feel free to reach out: sheryl.chang@berkeley.edu :-)
