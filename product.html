Product Design
Circle
SUMMER 2019
A class project I completed with Henry Hao, Purvi Ratnani, and Cathy Li.
Our initial prompt was to "design the future of conviviality." The result was Circle, a mindfulness app connecting you to your loved ones in times of both success and hardship.
OKurt
SUMMER 2019
A class project I completed with Teresa Pho, Dominic Hugo, and Avinash Reddy.
Our initial prompt was to "reimagine the breakfast experience." The result was OKurt, a small and convenient yogurt package designed to make your morning more relaxing.
Corgi-Bot
FALL 2018
A class project I completed with Ru-Ping Chen and Beatrice Bui.
Our initial prompt was to "design a civic-minded campus service." The result was Corgi-Bot, a mobile, corgi-shaped robot on the UC Berkeley campus that delivers physical messages to people.
