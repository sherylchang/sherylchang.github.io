100 Days of Design
MAY 2019 - JANUARY 2020
100 Days of Design is a personal project I started in May 2019 (the day of my last final heh) to get better at illustration and layout design! I was inspired by my dear friend, Meria Ogawa, to take on this incredible challenge.
My prompt for myself every day is: Create a layout design about your day or your life using vector graphics and/or type.
My designs are heavily inspired by Pinterest, my talented friend Meria, and another designer, Do-Hee Kim.
