Sugar & Ice
SPRING 2019
Sugar & Ice is a (hypothetical) homemade ice cream shop with a cutesy feel to it. This branding project consisted of a logo, tagline, business card, posters, and graphics. The pink color and curvy font were used to fit the theme of ice cream.
