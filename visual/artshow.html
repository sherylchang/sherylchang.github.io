My first art show
SPRING 2024
I had a little art table this May at my old climbing gym!! I showed and sold handmade mini bullet journals for June 2024 and prints of my digital designs and film photos. I'm probably going to open my own Etsy shop at some point, and this was a test project to see if people would like my products and style.
It turned out really well! A lot of my friends showed up to support me as well :') Stay tuned for my Etsy shop opening soon!!
