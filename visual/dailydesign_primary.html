100 Days of Design
MAY 2019 - JANUARY 2020
100 Days of Design is a personal project I started in May 2019 (the day of my last final that semester!) to get better at illustration and layout design! I was inspired by my talented friend, Meria Ogawa, to take on this incredible challenge.
My prompt for myself every day was: Create a layout design about your day or your life using vector graphics and/or typography.
Some of my designs are inspired by Pinterest, Meria, and Do-Hee Kim, who did a 100 Days of Typography project.
