Designs @ Genentech
Wine Bottle Labels
FALL 2019
My department is ordering wine bottles as end-of-year gifts, and I was asked to design a custom wine bottle label for us. Here are my initial designs.
I wanted to design something all-inclusive, not referencing any religious holidays, i.e. Christmas, Hanukkah, etc. This is why I only used "Season's Greetings" and "Happy Holidays" and why I went with snowman and ice skating themes.
I used mostly blues in my designs since I thought the cooler color scheme matched the winter season well. I used Petit Formal Script because I liked its fancy feel and thought it would look good on the wine bottles.
Final Design
My department liked the design with the people building the snowman the best :')
For the wine we ordered, the label size is different, so I had to resize my initial design.

Award Certificates
FALL 2019
My department is giving out end-of-year awards for the first time this year, and they asked me to make the certificate designs.
I wanted to try something different, so instead of using graphics to match each award category, I made 6 different certificates with abstract shapes and different color schemes.
